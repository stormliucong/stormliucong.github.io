CliGenAILab

Advancing clinical genetics/genomics with trustworthy AI.

At CliGenAILab, we explore the interface of clinical genetics and artificial intelligence to improve patient care and outcomes. We build methods and tools that translate cutting-edge research into actionable insights for clinicians and patients.

We collaborate with clinicians, geneticists, and data scientists to tackle complex challenges in healthcare—from developing predictive models for rare-disease diagnosis to creating agentic AI tools that support precision medicine.

We’re always looking for curious, mission-driven people to work with us. Interested in our research or a collaboration? Get in touch.

Research Interests

Current Projects

  • RESCUE: R01 funded project to train machine-learning–based models using EHR data and identify patients who may benefit from exome/genome sequencing (ES/GS).
  • Multimodal Diagnosis: In partnership with CHOP and Columbia, integrating genomic, facial-image, and clinical data to improve rare-disease diagnostics.
  • eMERGE: Studying polygenic risk scores and how to integrate them into the EHR to support clinical decision-making and improve outcomes.
  • Agentic AI for Clinical Genetics: Designing and evaluating agentic architectures that go beyond simple chatbots to execute end-to-end clinical genetics/genomics workflows.
  • PRICE: Building a cost-efficient analysis framework for AI-enabled rare-disease diagnosis.
  • Patient-Centered Medical AI: Assessing the feasibility of translating hospital-trained models into patient-facing apps in collaboration with third-party partners.
